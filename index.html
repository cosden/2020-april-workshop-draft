First RSE Workshop

We are excited to announce that through a generous grant from the Alfred P. Sloan Foundation, we will be able to hold the first US-RSE Association community building workshop, April 21-22, 2020 in Princeton, NJ. This will be a workshop focused on planning the best path forward to grow the US-RSE Association.. This workshop promises to be an important milestone in the growth of the US-RSE Association!


The workshop will have breakout sessions focused on a number of different topics pertinent to the US-RSE community.
